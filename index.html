FAQ

Questions, answered.

How fast is my VPS activated?
Orders are provisioned by our team immediately after payment — typically within minutes. Your server credentials appear in your client area and you'll be notified in your dashboard.
Which operating systems can I run?
Ubuntu, Debian, AlmaLinux, Rocky Linux and Windows Server 2022 are available on all plans. Need something else? Open a ticket and we'll load a custom ISO.
Is DDoS protection really included?
Yes — every plan ships with always-on volumetric DDoS mitigation at no extra cost. There is no "premium protection" upsell.
Can I upgrade my plan later?
Absolutely. Open a support ticket from your client area and we'll migrate you to a bigger plan with zero data loss — you only pay the difference.
What's your refund policy?
We offer a 48-hour money-back guarantee on first orders. If Reloaded VPS isn't for you, open a ticket and we'll refund you, no questions asked.
How do I get support?
Every client gets access to our 24/7 ticket desk directly inside the client area. Median first response time is under 15 minutes.
